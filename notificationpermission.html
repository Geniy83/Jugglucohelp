Notifications

Unless Juggluco is used with Freestyle Libre 0 sensors, Juggluco must remain active in the background to receive a glucose reading every minute either directly from the sensor via Bluetooth or via a network connection. To prevent apps from being killed by Android, Android requires apps to display a notification so users know the application is present in the background. The text “To connect with sensor” or “To exchange data” describes why Juggluco has to stay active in the background.

Juggluco also uses the notification to display the current glucose value and a change direction arrow.
