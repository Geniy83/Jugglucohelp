Android 13 requires that before you even know what kind of notifications are send, you give permissions for these notifications. Juggluco has notifications containing glucose values and requires to have a notifications in connection with a foreground service that is required to stay active in the background. For the proper function of Juggluco, you can better give Juggluco this permission.

You can easily turn notifications on and off in Androids settings App-Info -> Notifications for Juggluco.
