"Do not disturb" access

On some devices, alarms don't work during "Do not disturb" mode. With "Disturb" you can specify whether "Do not disturb" should be turned off before playing the alarm.

WARNING: Do not set "Disturb" if the alarm works when pressing "Play" during "Do not disturb". With "Disturb" set Juggluco will when an alarm goes off, turn off "Do not disturb" before playing the alarm. It will no longer turn on "Do not disturb" again, because this had the unintended side-effect that "Do not disturb" was not automatically turned off according to a set time schedule.
