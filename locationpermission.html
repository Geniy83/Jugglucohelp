Location permission

Devices running Android 6 to 11 need location permission to find Sibionics sensors.

Previously Juggluco needed location permission to find the Bluetooth device address of FreeStyle Libre 2 sensors. Now Juggluco uses another way to get the device address on most devices and is location permission not necessary.

Juggluco still uses the old method on Android 6,7 and 7.1 and location permission is still needed for them.
